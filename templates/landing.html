How It Works

1

Hand Detection

Our system uses MediaPipe to detect and track hand movements in real-time.

2

Landmark Extraction

21 key points on each hand are identified and normalized for processing.

3

Sign Recognition

Our trained ML model analyzes the landmarks to recognize ASL signs.

4

Subtitle Generation

Recognized signs are converted to text and displayed as subtitles.
